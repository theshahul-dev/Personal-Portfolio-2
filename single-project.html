Nexus

UX DESIGN

RESEARCH

Overview

For this project, I designed an app that allows users to explore AI-generated art. My goal was to create an app that would not only showcase the beauty and complexity of AI-generated art but also allow users to interact with it in a meaningful way. To achieve this, I conducted extensive user research, analyzing existing apps and interviewing art enthusiasts and collectors.

Solution

Based on this research, I designed an app that features a clean and modern interface with a focus on the art itself. The app includes a variety of filters and search options that allow users to easily discover new pieces of art and explore different themes and styles. I also incorporated a social aspect into the app, allowing users to share their favorite pieces of art with friends and connect with other art enthusiasts.

Result

Since launching the app, it has received positive feedback from both users and art critics, who have praised its clean and user-friendly interface as well as its ability to showcase the beauty and complexity of AI-generated art. The app has also been featured in several art publications and has gained a loyal following of art enthusiasts and collectors. Overall, I am proud of the work I did on this project and believe it demonstrates my ability to design user-centered experiences that meet the needs of specific user groups while also pushing the boundaries of what is possible with technology.
